## 🧠 About Me

10 years growing from Junior Developer to Lead Full Stack 
Engineer — architecting an entire company's technology 
infrastructure and contributing directly to scaling annual 
revenue from $2–3M to nearly $30M.

My work started where most developers won't go — deep inside 
legacy spaghetti PHP codebases that businesses depended on 
but couldn't maintain or grow. I led the full migration to 
structured MVC architecture using CodeIgniter and later 
Laravel, then architected the frontend evolution from jQuery 
to Vue.js across an entire ERP platform.
